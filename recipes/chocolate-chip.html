Gluten free chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Everyone needs a chocolate chip cookie recipe in their home. If your preference is eating a gluten free version of this traditional, American favorite dessert, then look no further.

Ingredients

  • Dry Ingredients
    • 1 cup oat flour
    • 1/2 tsp baking soda
    • 1/4 tsp salt
    • 1/2 cup coconut sugar
    • 1/3 cup chocolate chips
  • Wet Ingredients
    • 2 tbsp milk of choice
    • 2 tbsp melted butter
    • 1/4 tsp pure vanilla extract

    Instructions

    1. Combine all dry ingredients in a bowl, then stir in wet ingredients to form a dough(if needed add 1-2 tbsp extra milk).
    2. Form into one big ball, then either refrigerate at least 2 hours or freeze untill dough is cold.
    3. Once dough is chilled, preheat oven to 325F or 160C.
    4. Form dough balls and place on a baking tray that is greased or lined with a baking sheet.
    5. Bake 11 minutes. They'll look underdone when you take them out. Let them cool on the baking tray for 10 minutes before touching.
    6. If cookies don't spread enough while baking, press them down with a spoon when you take them out.
