Creamy Vegetable Soup

Description

This is a nutritious, delicious, creamy soup that is easy to make. We make this recipe usually 1-2 times per week as an easy way to get our vegetables in! As time goes on preparing it usually takes us no time at all.

To make this deilicious recipe, all you need are vegetables that you can find in any market, 5-10 minutes for prepping and an appetite.

Ingredient list

  • 1 big white onion
  • 5 sticks of celery
  • 5-6 carrots (depending on size)
  • 1 lbs (around 500 g) potatoes
  • 3-4 garlic cloves
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp thyme (fresh or dried)
  • salt and pepper
  • olive oil
  • 3 cups water or stock
  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt
  • optional: turmeric, red pepper flakes

Insructions

  1. Roughly chop all the vegetables. I usually chop them in big chunks(around 1/2 inch) to minimize prepping time, but chop them small enough so you can blend them later on.
  2. On medium-high heat add just enough olive oil to cover the bottom of the pot. After it heats up add the onion, carrots and the celery. Cook them around 4-5 minutes or untill the onion starts to sweat.
  3. Add the potatoes and the garlic and keep cooking for 1-2 minutes. Then add bay leaves and thyme.
  4. Pour the water or the stock in, just enough to cover the vegetables. Add salt and pepper. Bring to a boil, then lower to simmer and cook untill you can easily pierce the vegetables with a fork, about 20 minutes.
  5. When the vegetables are softened, remove the pot from the heat. Remove the bay leaves and the thyme sping if using fresh thyme.
  6. Puree the soup until smooth with a hand blender or a normal blender.
  7. Stir in the yogurt. Add salt and pepper if needed.
