Swedish Meatballs

Description

Have you ever tried the swedish meatballs in Ikea and liked them so much you wanted to have them again? Well this time you don't have to go all the way to Ikea to get them, you can easily make them at the comfort of your own home.

This recipe might look complicated, but it's actually not that hard to make. All you need is some time and a will to cook!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (500 grams) ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup flour, almond flour or gluten free flour
  • 2-3 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon allspice
  • 4 cups stock or water
  • 4 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup yogurt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 250C or 480F
  2. Add the ground beef, chopped onion, egg, flour, yogurt, salt, black pepper, nutmeg and allspice to a large bowl. Mix everything thoroughly together using your hands and form into rounds.
  3. Place the meatballs onto a baking sheet. Bake for 10 minutes. Flip over and bake for another 4-5 minutes.
  4. Place the meatballs into a medium sized pot. Add the stock or water and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat and simmer for an hour.
  6. Mix the cornstarch with 4 tablespoons water. Slowly add it into the pot while gently stirring.
  7. Once the sauce has thickened, remove from heat and add the yogurt.
